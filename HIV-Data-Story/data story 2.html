HIV Diagnoses Continue to Rise in New York City for the Fourth Year in a Row

By Benjamin Flynn

On December 1, 2025, the New York City Department of Health and Mental Hygiene released its annual HIV Surveillance Report, showing that HIV diagnoses continue to rise across the city. The findings mark a significant reversal from the steady decline in HIV rates seen prior to 2020.
